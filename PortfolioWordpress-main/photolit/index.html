This project was part of Photo-Lit in collaboration with Katholieke Universiteit Leuven (KUL) and The Royal Library of Belgium (KBR). More info on Photo-Lit can be found here.

The goal was to create a re-enactment of a Belgian photonovel from the open repository of KBR for a closing exhibition of the research project Photo-lit. With a group of three students (Lia Herbots, Jacqui Li and me) we contacted a professional photographer (Pieter Janssens) and set up a production crew and team of actors. Together we created a five-episode rendition of the photonovel ‘Een eenvoudig verhaal’. The original photonovel can be found on the website of KBR.

A printed version of the re-enactment was limited to 3000 copies, and was available for free to visitors of the exhibition. The exhibition itself ran from 19/03/2021-08/05/2021. More information about the exhibition can be found here.
