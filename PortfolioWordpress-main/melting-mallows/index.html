Melting Mallows

Blues Rock, Rock ‘n Roll

Band

Melting Mallows is a rock band from Leuven, originally consisting of Brian Pittoors and Bices Mali. I joined the band for a time to accompany on bass guitar. The band won a belgian music concours called Soundtrack and became finalist in another major concours De Nieuwe Lichting. We played several shows together.

Studio Tonzofia

Our rehearsel room (see video above) was at a semi-professional studio, Tonzofia, and is owned by one of the bandmembers (Brian). Many other band come record here and work together with the studio to release music. We also recorded our demo songs of my other band Nona Problemo at this location. This gave me the opportunity to learn more about studio equipment and learn to work with it.
